## About The Project

Flum is a clone of Yelp a popular online directory for discovering local businesses. Flum where users are able to create their own business, and for users to be able to review any business they've had experience with. This Project was done over the course of 2 weeks during my time at App Academy boot camp.

Upon first entering the site, you will be able to view 3 random business that is available on the webpage also some of the most recent reviews that were left on the page. The site is completely viewable even if you are not logged in, but in order to leave a review or create a business you must be logged in, you also have the option to log in as a demo user under the login and signup page.

![Screen Shot 2022-12-01 at 4 20 20 PM](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/107518826/205186525-0a4e00be-b92d-4ef5-9a20-785dbe9bdb00.png)

On The top-right-hand corner, there are also two links available for anyone visiting the site. "For Businesses" and "write a review", If you are not logged in "for businesses" will redirect you to the login page, where you will also have the option to sign up for an account if you wish. If you are logged in, "for businesses" will instead redirect you to the create new business form, where you can create a business of your own. "write a review" will always direct you to search bar page, where you can search for business you want to create a review for.

Not Logged in (for businesses)
![Screen Shot 2022-12-01 at 4 25 20 PM](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/107518826/205187105-89b7b4b0-fad4-407f-a8eb-58ac51a34958.png)

Logged in (for businesses)

![Screen Shot 2022-12-01 at 4 26 30 PM](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/107518826/205187245-a7fbe878-7ae7-47d6-93ff-9b5b0823dc01.png)

To navigate the page, you can either search for a business name you know that is available on the page, or you can click on any of the business names that are shown on the main page. Once you enter the business details page. Depending on your connection to the business, you will see a few different large red buttons available. If you are the owner of the business the 'edit Business' and 'delete Business" buttons will be available to you. If you are just a regular user the "Write a review' button will be available.

![Screen Shot 2022-12-01 at 4 30 54 PM](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/107518826/205187672-fc3ab301-55ba-41b4-a9ba-f18be0a07f36.png)

Once you enter the write a review page, you then can rate the business using the stars rating given, and leave any review comment you wish. You also have the option to add photo for your review if you wish.
